# FocusRoom

A focus-room app for remote workers that pairs two people into a silent,
camera-on 50-minute deep-work session. You state your one goal for the block,
work in parallel with a stranger (no talking), then take a 10-minute break
together. The presence of another person creates accountability — like a gym
class for focus.

- **Target users:** remote knowledge workers, freelancers, ADHD / accountability-seekers
- **Market:** productivity / remote-work tools
- **Stage:** pre-mvp
